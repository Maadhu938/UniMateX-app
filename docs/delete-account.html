Delete Your Account & Data

UniMateX · Account deletion request

You can permanently delete your UniMateX account and all associated data at any time. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

Delete from inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open UniMateX and go to the Profile screen (tap your avatar on the Home screen).
  2. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm by tapping Confirm Delete. You may be asked to sign in again for security.

Your account is then removed and you are signed out.

Can't access the app? Email us from the address linked to your account at support@unimatex.app with the subject "Delete my UniMateX account". We will verify your request and delete your account within 7 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account credentials (email / Google sign-in link).
  • All cloud data: subjects, attendance records, timetable, assignments, and notes.
  • The local cache stored on your device.

Data retention

When you delete your account, your personal data and academic data are removed from our active systems immediately. Residual copies in encrypted backups (if any) are purged within 30 days. We do not retain your data for any other purpose after deletion.

Questions

Contact us at support@unimatex.app.
